Hello, author.
This is your page.

We publish books written by young authors, not for them, by them. Use this form to send us your story. We'll edit it the way a careful friend would: only enough to keep the reader from getting stuck, and never enough to change your voice.

01  The deal

We keep your voice.

Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, the way your dadi says it. We don't iron out the way you write.

02  What you need

About twenty minutes.

Your story, a few questions, a clear photo (if you want one). A grown-up signs off at the end.

03  What happens next

An editor writes back.

Within a fortnight you'll hear from a real human. Light edits only. We always ask before changing anything.
